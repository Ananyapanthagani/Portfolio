How I Work

A simple, repeatable process.

No theatre, no guesswork. Every project moves through these four stages — whether it's a 3-screen concept or a full product.

01
Research
Interviews, reviews, competitor audits. I start by listening — not sketching.
02
Define
Turn findings into clear problems worth solving, with success criteria I can measure.
03
Design
Wireframes to high-fidelity Figma prototypes, mobile-first, with WCAG compliance built in.
04
Deliver
Hand off clean design files and, when needed, build the frontend myself in HTML/React.
About

UX Designer with a developer's mindset.

I'm a junior UX Designer based in Calgary, Canada. My path into design began through software development at SAIT Polytechnic, where I learned how digital products are built — and realized I was far more interested in how they feel to use.

I approach every project with a research-first mindset. I believe good UX comes from listening carefully, questioning assumptions, and iterating often. My dual background in design and development means I create interfaces that are not only thoughtful and accessible, but genuinely buildable.

"Great design isn't about what I think looks good — it's about what actually works for the people using it."

I'm actively building my portfolio through self-directed concept work and pursuing my Google UX Design Certificate. I'm seeking my first full-time UX role or contract opportunity where I can contribute real value while growing under experienced designers.
